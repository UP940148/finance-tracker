About Us

Student Budgets is a free budgeting site which is set up to help students save every penny. Who doesn’t like the extra few coins at the end of the month? The site searches for some of the best discounted deals on the internet, which will enable students to use these discount codes and make a saving in their purchase. You are one-click away from making a saving. Students Budgets has automatically directed discounted links to making sites such as Spotify for the lovers, all the way to Apple for the technology gurus. Searches for and tests available coupon codes at checkout on over 10,000+ popular sites. If we find a working code, we then apply the one with the biggest savings to your cart like magic. Ta da! Student Budgets can even search for better prices on Amazon. Who knew? Well, we did. And now so do you. Thanks to Student Budgets, you can earn rewards just for shopping on participating sites. We’re always adding new stores and features so stay tuned.
